Our Story

DreamWithCode is a Community Based Organization(C.B.0) founded with the aim of reaching out to children, especially those from underprivileged families, and teaching them how to write computer programs. The vision shared by the founders of the organization was to equip a generation with programming skills at an early age.

The organization brings together young energetic experts in computing-related fields with the primary aim of providing a platform where they can impart their knowledge and experience while giving back to society and impacting the lives of the young ones. At DreamWithCode, we channel the immense creativity, dedication, and cohesion, from our team towards our mission.

Our Core Values

“Coding is the language of the future, and every girl should learn it. As I've learned from watching girls grow and learn in our classrooms, coding is fun, collaborative and creative.” - Reshma Saujani

Mission

To be the leading transformer of the lives of the young generation especially those living in the Kenyan Slums by teaching them computer programming skills.

Vision

To empower children and teenagers with the power code to build better lives for themselves and their families through computing and computer programming.

Objective

To reach out to as many underprivileged kids within society and teach them how to leverage technology to make better lives for themselves.
